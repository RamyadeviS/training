         E-pass Mandatory In Tamilnadu



    1. COVID-19 has turned our lives upside down. To control the spread of the virus, the government has imposed a lockdown in the state of Tamilnadu.


    2. As a result, people are not allowed to step out of their homes unless it is necessary.


    3. Even for essential tasks like going to the grocery store or the doctor, people need to obtain an e-pass from the government.


    4.The Tamilnadu State government has made TN
    E-Pass Registration compulsory for traveling anywhere in India because of the increase in COVID-19 Fresh Cases.
